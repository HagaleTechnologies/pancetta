## What ships

1. **`MAX_SYNC_CANDIDATES: 300 → 200`** (decoder.rs default). Recall cost
   ≤0.03% on both corpora for −7% FPs and ~1.5× decode speed. This
   reverts hb-038's 2026-05-23 raise (200→300, claimed +96 TPs on
   hard_1000 under pancetta truth): on neutral truth the step measures
   +3..+5 TPs / +260..+653 FPs. Third pancetta-truth-era default
   corrected by neutral-truth audit (osd_depth, B56 magnitude, now this).
2. **Slow-tier preset → `max_sync_candidates = 150`** (tier.rs): bigger
   trade (−0.06..−0.15% recall, −16% FPs) justified by 2.3× decode speed
   on underpowered hardware.
3. **Slow-tier preset DROPS `osd_depth = Some(1)`** — a stale interaction
   caught during wiring: that rewrite predates Batch 72 and was a
   *reduction* from the Some(2)-era default; on top of today's Some(0)
   default it would *raise* OSD depth (+77 FPs / +1 TP per the Batch 72
   hard_1000 sweep). Slow tier now inherits the default Some(0).
