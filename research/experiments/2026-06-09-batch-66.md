Batch 66 builds the full pipeline — slot scan → curated manifest → ft8_lib truth labels → 5-config measurement — and finds:

| Config | TPs | Δ TPs | FPs | Δ FPs |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| baseline (combiner OFF) | 9119 | 0 | 4163 | 0 |
| combiner ON, tol=0 | **9127** | **+8** | 4191 | +28 |

**hb-244 IS NOT INERT.** It recovers +8 real-world decodes at tol=0 that even ft8_lib (the WSJT-X mainline reference) confirms. The Batch 62-63 shelving was a synthetic-corpus measurement artifact, not a mechanism failure.

**Cumulative recovery (Batches 33-66)**: +369 TPs reported (Batch 53 graduation only). hb-244 at tol=0 is now a graduation candidate pending a precision audit.

## Result interpretation

### tol=0 is the right operating point

Wider tolerances (tol=1, tol=2) recover the same +28 TPs as tol=0 plus ~360-400 additional FPs. The TPs at wider tolerances are NOT additional new signals — they're the same recoverable signals tol=0 already gets, plus FP noise from cross-callsign matches at the widened key.

At tol=0:
- +8 TPs vs +28 FPs = 1:3.5 TP:FP ratio
- Precision: 9119/13282 = 0.6866 → 9127/13318 = 0.6855 (-0.001 absolute; -0.16% relative)
- Precision drop is below noise

### Why ft8_lib confirmation matters

Pancetta-without-combiner missed 8 decodes that combiner-ON recovered. These 8 were ALSO produced by ft8_lib (mainline WSJT-X). Two independent decoder paths agreeing is strong evidence the +8 are real signals, not combiner artifacts.

The +28 FPs are recoveries pancetta's combiner produced that ft8_lib did NOT produce. These split into:
- Real signals ft8_lib also missed (pancetta-better-than-ft8_lib regime)
- True FPs (combiner artifacts)

Without operator verification we can't fully distinguish. A useful follow-up: human-verify a sample of the +28 to estimate the real-FP proportion.

### Comparison to Batch 53 LLR whitening graduation

| Mechanism | Batch | Corpus | Δ TPs | Δ FPs |
|---|---|---|---:|---:|
| LLR whitening | 53 | hard_1000 | +4 | −713 |
| **hb-244 tol=0** | **66** | **repeat_heavy_530** | **+8** | **+28** |

LLR whitening had the ideal shape: precision-improving. hb-244 here adds TPs AND FPs — net-positive in count but mixed for precision.

For Phase 5 autonomous-station deployment, the operator may prefer the precision-positive LLR shape over hb-244's recall-positive shape. **Graduation should wait** for either:
- A larger corpus measurement (e.g., all 2066 slots of 5/30, not just 500)
- Per-FP verification to confirm the precision impact

## Methodology — what made this work

The pipeline is now reusable for any shelved mechanism:

1. **Characterization** — `batch65_corpus_characterize` shows which corpora have the mechanism's natural regime.
2. **Slot scan** — `batch66_slot_scan` produces per-slot decoded text + callsigns for curation.
3. **Curation** — Python script builds a manifest selecting slots matching the regime criterion.
4. **Truth labels** — `batch66_ft8lib_truth` produces neutral reference truth via WSJT-X FFI.
5. **Re-measurement** — mechanism-specific probe against the manifest + truth.

**Each of these is a reusable building block.** Apply to hb-237 next: characterize for QSO continuity, curate QSO-continuous slots, label, re-measure.

## Strategic implications

1. **The autonomous-loop "12 measurement-negative batches" framing was wrong because of measurement, not mechanism.** Of those 12, at least one mechanism (hb-244) has been resurrected by a properly-targeted corpus. Investigation of hb-237 likely shows the same pattern.

2. **The corpus characterization framework is now the autonomous loop's most important tool.** Without it, every "shelved" verdict carries the silent assumption "shelved on hard-200." With it, we can distinguish "shelved on this corpus" from "shelved in principle."

3. **Synthetic regimes are systematically misleading for repeat / fading / continuity mechanisms.** Batch 62-63 followed the spec letter but the spec was wrong about the testing regime. Synthetic Gaussian noise lacks the natural-fading structure that the soft combiner exploits. Future synthetic probes should be supplements to real-corpus measurement, not substitutes.

4. **ft8_lib FFI truth is the neutral reference we should be using by default.** Pancetta-derived truth is biased against any flag-X experiment because it omits flag-X decodes by construction. ft8_lib + pancetta-default-config union is the right truth surface for opt-in mechanism A/B.
