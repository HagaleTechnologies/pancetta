## Why re-check

hb-086 V2 (soft joint LLR with iterative interference cancellation)
was SHELVED at the diagnostic gate on 2026-05-30 (batch 15) because
top-20 hard-200 worst-WAVs showed 0% of V1-failed candidates with a
marginal-SNR neighbor (median neighbor SNR -1.5 dB, nothing below
-15 dB). The conclusion: when neighbors are uniformly strong, their
LDPC-corrected codewords yield delta-function tone posteriors and
soft cancellation collapses to hard subtraction.

The corpus refresh ingestion (2026-05-31) added 100 today's K5ARH
20m WAVs to hard-200, and the corpus survey noted that 9 of 20
sample slots reach jt9's -25 dB SNR floor — exactly the marginal-SNR
regime V2 was theorized to need. A re-check on the refreshed top-20
was the cheapest experiment that could either unshelve V2 or
strengthen the SHELVE.

## Method

Same `examples/hb086_v2_soft_posterior_potential.rs` diagnostic as
the original shelve. Reads top-20 worst-WAVs from `main.json`'s
`per_wav_top_failures` (now reflecting refreshed corpus). For each
missed truth with at least one nearby pancetta decode (the V1
pair-likely subset), counts (a) multi-neighbor cases (≥2 nearby
decodes within window) and (b) marginal-SNR-among-neighbors cases
(any nearby decode with SNR < -15 dB).

Decision thresholds (unchanged from spec):
- Primary: ≥20% marginal-SNR among V1-failed → PROCEED
- Secondary: ≥20% multi-neighbor → PROCEED (count-only)

## Structural insight (this is the keep)

**Pancetta's *decoded* neighbors are uniformly high-confidence, even
when the band has marginal-SNR *truths*.** When the decoder produces
a neighbor, that neighbor passed CRC-14 verification — which selects
for sharp LLRs. The soft posterior of a CRC-passing decode is
indistinguishable from its hard projection for V2's purposes.

This is a property of pancetta's **decoder architecture** (hard-decision
pipeline through LDPC + CRC), not the **corpus**. No band condition,
no time-of-day, no fading regime will produce a different pattern:
pancetta's filtered-output decode pool is by construction high-confidence.

## Implications for the V2 / hb-081 family

- **hb-086 V2 (soft joint LLR)**: DEFINITIVELY SHELVED. Mechanism is
  structurally closed against pancetta's decoded neighborhood.
- **hb-081 (per-decode subtract scaling)**: shelved 2026-05-27 with
  -170 hard-200 regression. Same root cause — under-subtracting a
  CRC-validated decode discards reliable energy.
- **Any future hypothesis** that reasons about pancetta's *decoded*
  neighbors via soft/probabilistic methods will hit the same wall.
  The hard-decision pipeline upstream truncates the tone-uncertainty
  signal these mechanisms need.
