## Telemetry semantics

| Field | Populated from | Range | Meaning |
|---|---|---:|---|
| `bp_iterations_used` | `belief_propagation_with_features` convergence iteration | `1..=ldpc_iterations` (clamped to u8 max 255) | Lower = BP converged fast = strong signal |
| `min_llr_magnitude` | `min_i \|output_llrs[i]\|` over 174-bit codeword | `[0.0, +∞)` | Higher = high confidence; near 0 = bit was a coin-flip |
| `osd_depth_used` | (Session 3 — not yet populated) | `0..=4` | OSD depth at which codeword accepted |
| `nharderrs` | (Session 3 — not yet populated) | `0..=u8::MAX` | Hard-decision bit errors OSD corrected |

The FDR-relevant convention: **lower bp_iterations_used + higher min_llr_magnitude = higher decode confidence**. The FDR module in Session 4 will combine these (plus the Session 3 OSD features) into per-message-type confidence thresholds.
