## Root cause

`compute_spectrogram` row `t`'s analysis window is centred at
`(t−1)·960` (frame ends at `(t+1)·960`), so the represented symbol
starts at `(t−2)·960`; all conversions assumed `t·960`. Fix:
`SLIDING_FRAME_LOOKBACK_STEPS = 2` + `candidate_offset_samples()`
helper, `reverse_derive_candidate` exact inverse.
