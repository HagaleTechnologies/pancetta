## Reads

1. **The aligned subtract works**: multipass payloads roughly doubled on
   raw (both TPs and FPs) — Batch 88's prediction that the mechanism was
   structurally hobbled is confirmed mechanically.
2. **But the economics don't improve**: what the extra passes recover at
   the margin is FP-dominated at the same-or-worse rate. The recall the
   subtract unlocks is mostly already captured by the default config's
   coherent-multipass rounds.
3. **The dt fix itself improved the default**: +2 TPs (raw) / +12 TPs
   (hard_1000) at mp=1 — the default's 3 coherent-multipass rounds
   benefit from aligned subtraction. (The accompanying +70/+92 raw-scored
   "FPs" include both hash artifacts and the genuine beyond-ft8_lib
   decodes the Batch 88 guard audited.)
4. **Fast-preset retirement (B83) stands** — and now rests on a
   measurement free of the alignment confound.
