**Key findings**:
- **`ldpc_iterations=200` is the cleanest ship**: +16 TPs at modest WC
  cost (LDPC early-terminates so avg iter rises modestly). Plateaus
  at 400 (+10), so 200 is the Pareto-optimal value.
- **Current OSD depth=2 is optimally tuned**: lower (None/0/1) costs
  ~0-1 TPs; higher (3/4) explodes FPs without recovering TPs.
- **Dither universally negative**: pancetta's spectrogram is already
  well-shaped; adding noise doesn't help LDPC escape local minima.
- **DC offset removal +4 TPs**: small positive; could ship as a
  standalone change. Deferred to Batch 42.
- **Pre-emphasis negative**: counterproductive at the tested shelf shape.
  Worth re-trying with different shelf parameters.

## Research — web findings (highlights)

### Competing decoders / forks

- **ft8mon** (rtmrtmrtmrtm / AB1HL): experimental FT8 decoder with
  several mechanisms pancetta hasn't tried:
  - Fractional sub-bin Costas search (`coarse_hz_n=4 × coarse_off_n=4`)
  - A-priori bit probability prior (`apriori174[]` hardcoded array)
  - Conditional OSD gating (only invoke OSD when BP terminates with
    ≥70/83 correct parity bits)
  - Three-stage sync refinement (coarse → fine → very-fine)
  - Rate-reduction preprocessing (~100 Hz downsample per candidate)
- **WSJT-X Improved v3.0.0/3.1.0**:
  - MTD (multi-threaded) + STD (single-threaded) cooperation via
    shared hash table
  - Auto-optimized passband baseline calculation
  - 4th pass after a7 decoding
- **JTDX**: "subpass" (second AF spectrum pass with different settings)
  and "QSO partner decoding filter" (narrow-band targeting around
  a specific weak signal — user-driven; pancetta could automate)
- **WSJT-X 2.2 multi-interval decoding**: FT8 decodes at 11.8s / 13.5s /
  14.7s into the 15s slot; first interval yields ~85% of total decodes.
  Pancetta does single full-slot decode. **+10% on crowded bands** per
  ARRL release notes.

### Sync threshold cascading — important finding

WSJT-X's `ft8_decode.f90` **lowers sync threshold BETWEEN passes
(2.1 → 1.3)** to pick up weaker signals revealed by subtraction.
Pancetta has the knob (`Ft8Config::residual_min_sync_score:
Option<f64>`, hb-082) but the default is `None` = reuses production
3.0 across all passes.

**This was NEVER ablated at the global level.** hb-086 V3's
sync relaxation was in a LOCALIZED window only. A global threshold
cascade (e.g., `residual_min_sync_score = Some(1.5)`) is a
high-priority untried probe for Batch 42.

### Academic literature (2023-2026)

- **ORBGRAND** and **Reshuffling-ORBGRAND** for short-block LDPC —
  capacity-achieving, near-ML at modest overhead. (174,87) is
  squarely in scope. arxiv 2507.08696, 2401.15946.
- **SGRAND** as ML oracle — slow but quantifies how much BP+OSD is
  leaving on the table. arxiv 1607.01827.
- **Neural min-sum LDPC** — learned BP matches OSD on short codes
  at 5-10× lower iter count. arxiv 2406.19664.
- **Adaptive normalized min-sum scaling** — 0.2-0.3 dB gain expected
  from learning the scaling factor per-iteration.
- **GPU-OSD at order 4-6** — May 2026 wsjt-devel thread; could give
  meaningful FER curve improvement at marginal SNR.
- **CrossMPT transformer decoder** — newest direction; best-in-class
  for 6G short codes; code-agnostic. arxiv 2507.01038.
