## Why ft8_lib found +9 TPs but pancetta truth only +4

Both numbers are positive, but ft8_lib's lift is 2.25× pancetta's. Plausible explanations:

1. **ft8_lib is more conservative** (13027 truth decodes vs pancetta's ~16365). Lower truth recall means each pancetta TP/FP boundary slot has fewer truth-matched neighbors. So mechanism changes have more leverage at the margin.

2. **ft8_lib's truth distribution is closer to "what would a fair reference produce"** than pancetta-self-truth. Pancetta-truth includes decodes only pancetta produces; those decodes biased the +4 number lower because pancetta-with-whitening doesn't necessarily produce the same pancetta-only decodes.

3. **Statistical variance**. +4 vs +9 are both small absolute numbers; the difference may be within noise.

Whichever explanation is right, the SIGN is consistent and the precision benefit is robust.
