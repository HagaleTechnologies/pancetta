# Batch 79 — hb-103 v3 feature validation: decode-lateness real, cross-day trust redundant

**Date**: 2026-06-11
**Branch**: `iter/2026-06-11-batch-78`
**Status**: COMPLETE — split-half AUC validation of the two hb-103 v2-candidate features. One survives (decode_time, +0.032/+0.012 held-out ΔAUC), one is redundant (hb-246 cross-day trust, weight ≈ 0). `content_score_v3_from_features` shipped to pancetta-qso (opt-in; production gate unchanged — see Batch 80).

## Method

Probe: `pancetta-research/examples/batch79_hb103_v3_features.rs`. Decode
hard_200 + raw_530 subset-200 (ft8_lib truth), compute v2 score and the two
feature values per decode, grid-search additive weights on even-indexed
WAVs, report AUC on odd-indexed WAVs (and vice versa). Headline = mean
held-out ΔAUC — fit and eval never share WAVs. Wide grid (−8…+1) confirmed
the optimum isn't edge-pinned: w_time = −1.0 won every fold.

## Results

| Corpus | v2 AUC | solo decode_time (inv) | solo trust | held-out Δ (corpus-max norm) | held-out Δ (slot-max norm) |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| hard_200 | 0.874 | 0.72-0.73 | 0.490 | +0.023…+0.028 | **+0.032** |
| raw_530 subset | 0.828 | 0.62-0.63 | 0.493 | +0.017…+0.023 | **+0.012** |

1. **decode_time_into_window is a real ranking feature**, confirming
   Diagnostic V at the combined-score level. The lift survives the
   production-feasible slot-max normalization on both corpora.
2. **hb-246 cross-day trust adds nothing on top of v2**: solo AUC ≈ 0.49
   (decode-level), fitted weight ≈ 0 in every fold. v2's continuity-trust
   input already captures the callsign-trust signal; the cross-day variant
   is redundant for scoring. (Batch 76's separation finding stands — it
   measured the *raw* signal, not the marginal value over v2.)
3. **Run-to-run instability observed**: the feature is wall-clock, so AUC
   numbers moved between runs under different CPU load (e.g. corpus-max Δ
   +0.0229 → +0.0276 on identical inputs). The lift persisted through the
   noise, but this drove Batch 80's decision to defer the gate flip to a
   deterministic provenance feature.
