# Batch 86 — hb-104 kill-switch: SHELVED as premise artifact; two high-value spawns

**Date**: 2026-06-12
**Branch**: `iter/2026-06-12-batch-86`
**Status**: COMPLETE — the kill-switch experiment ran and returned the strongest possible negative: the target population doesn't exist. The Batch 85 premise number was a truth-scoring artifact. hb-104 SHELVED-PREMISE-ARTIFACT; hb-248 (hash-normalized scoring, now highest open at 0.75) and hb-249 (systematic dt offset) spawned.

## What happened

Scoping spec (`docs/superpowers/specs/2026-06-12-hb104-joint-decode-scoping.md`)
defined a pre-registered kill-switch: one-step LS subtract of the decoded
co-channel neighbor + whole-residual re-decode on the top-20 slots by
<6.25 Hz miss-pair density (70 pairs). Implementer agent built and ran it
(`batch86_hb104_kill_switch.rs`, commit 5f60a28c).

Result: 0/54 tried pairs recovered — because **all 54 were hash-display
aliases**: `miss_message` and `neighbor_text` are the same decode rendered
differently (`<...>` by ft8_lib vs `<...NNNN>` by pancetta). Subtracting
the neighbor removes the "missed" signal itself. The in-program premise
audit over all 2,066 slots: 766/1,766 nominal misses (43.4%) are aliases —
matching Batch 85's 46.5% almost exactly. **Hash-normalized, 0/1,000
genuine misses sit within 6.25 Hz of a truth-confirmed decode** (6 within
25 Hz). The joint-decode population is empty on this corpus.

## What survives the wreckage

1. **The LS-subtract machinery is validated**: per-block amplitudes +
   fine-shift grid drove victim-band residual energy to 0.26 mean (best
   <0.05). Reusable for hb-090 / subtract-quality work.
2. **hb-248 (0.75, highest open)**: hash-normalize truth scoring. Every
   ft8_lib-truth measurement (B66-86) double-penalized hashed decodes
   (+1 phantom miss, +1 phantom FP each). True 5/30 miss rate is 2.5%,
   not 4.5%. Ship decisions unaffected (config-vs-config deltas under
   identical scoring) but all absolute precision numbers are understated.
3. **hb-249 (0.45)**: reported decode `time_offset` is systematically
   0.03-0.18 s late vs sample-accurate signal position (dt-scan locked
   -360 to -2160 samples on every probed slot). Invisible to LDPC, fatal
   to coherent processing, and plausibly degrading production subtract
   quality today.

## Methodological lessons

- **Inspect sample texts before promoting a population finding.** The
  Batch 85 diagnostic reported a distribution without printing a single
  example pair; one glance at `<...>` vs `<JA3ABC>` would have caught it.
  Batch 85's journal carries a correction note.
- The implementer's deviations were all correct calls (tone_symbols over
  text re-encode for hashed messages; 1500 Hz anchor + Hilbert
  heterodyne for the modulator's frequency ceiling; alias tagging that
  turned a null result into a diagnosis). Adversarial-by-default
  implementers are worth their tokens.
