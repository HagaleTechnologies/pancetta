## Why split FDR into 4 sessions

The WSJT-X Improved FDR spec (`research/specs/spec-wsjtx-improved-fdr.md`) estimates ~200 LOC + 160 LOC tests across 3-4 sessions. The decomposition:

| Session | Scope | LOC estimate | Risk |
|---|---|---:|---|
| **1** (this batch) | Type scaffolding: struct + DecodedMessage field + default-None | ~60 | Byte-identical; zero risk |
| 2 | BP plumbing: `belief_propagation_with_features` returning iterations_used + min_llr_magnitude | ~80 | Touches LDPC hot loop; needs careful benchmark |
| 3 | OSD plumbing: track `osd_depth_used` + `nharderrs` from the OSD path; wire one primary call site | ~60 | Touches OSD; coverage needs hard-200 |
| 4 | FDR module: `pancetta-qso/src/fdr.rs` with per-message-type confidence gate + tier defaults + calibration on hard-200 | ~200 | Production behavior change; bootstrap-CI gate required |

Session 1 lets Sessions 2+ proceed without needing further `DecodedMessage` schema changes. The split keeps each batch small, measurable, and revertable.

## Why ConfidenceFeatures is broadly useful (not just FDR)

The fields it captures (`bp_iterations_used`, `osd_depth_used`, `nharderrs`, `min_llr_magnitude`) are diagnostic signals that several pancetta paths could consume beyond FDR:

- **hb-103 content scoring**: currently scores on `(trust_set, snr, dt)`. Adding `min_llr_magnitude` as a feature should improve the FP/TP separation, especially in low-SNR regime.
- **Autonomous TX gating**: per-decode "confidence" is currently `confidence: f32` collapsed from one signal; the structured features give the operator far more diagnostic surface for tier-aware behaviors.
- **Research scorecard**: every probe currently computes `tps / decodes` for precision. Per-decode `bp_iterations_used` lets us see which TPs are "easy" (BP converged fast) vs "hard" (BP near max iterations). Future probes can stratify analyses by decode difficulty.

So even if FDR itself doesn't graduate (recall: 8 of 12 implementer-thread mechanisms have been "faithful but inapplicable"), the ConfidenceFeatures type pays its way through other consumers.
