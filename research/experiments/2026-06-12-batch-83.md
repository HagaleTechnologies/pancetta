# Batch 83 — multipass audit: Fast-tier preset RETIRED (mp=2/ldpc=200 was a pancetta-truth-era FP factory)

**Date**: 2026-06-12
**Branch**: main (direct)
**Status**: PRODUCTION SHIP — the Fast hardware tier's `Ft8Config` rewrite (`max_decode_passes=2, ldpc_iterations=200`, from Batch 36 B1 / Batch 41) is retired; Fast and Moderate tiers now run plain defaults. Completes the knob-audit arc: every default-reachable `Ft8Config` knob has now been measured under ft8_lib truth.

## Why this audit

The Fast preset rested on pancetta-truth-era claims (+32 TPs from mp=2,
+16 TPs from ldpc=200, both hard-200). Every other claim from that era
that has been re-measured under neutral truth shrank or inverted
(osd_depth, hb-038 cands raise, B56 Slow-tier whitening, hb-244). This
was the last unaudited one — and the most consequential, because the Fast
tier is what the production M4 actually runs.

nothing further.
