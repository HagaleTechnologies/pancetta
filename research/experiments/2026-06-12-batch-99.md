## Why synthetic-real divergence (third occurrence of the pattern)

The synthetic plant is single-signal AWGN — the paper's exact channel
model, where block-constant (Es, N0) is correct. hard_200 is
interference-dominated: block-constant N0 under-prices
interference-hit symbols, and the exact metric's extra confidence on
those symbols becomes wrong-codeword BP convergence. The re-attack
path is interference-aware per-symbol/per-tone noise pricing —
hb-259's EM re-estimation (priority raised 0.40 → 0.50; the Bessel
demapper + LSE SOMAP infrastructure shipped this batch is exactly the
consumer it needs).
