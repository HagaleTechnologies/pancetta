## BB-track — Mechanism probes

### BB1 — Δdt distribution

For each frontier entry, `|truth.dt - neighbor.dt|`:

| Δdt | count | % |
|---|---:|---:|
| 0-0.1s | 269 | 27.6% |
| 0.1-0.3s | 396 | 40.7% |
| 0.3-0.5s | 127 | 13.0% |
| 0.5-1.0s | 158 | 16.2% |
| 1.0-1.5s | 24 | 2.5% |

**68% of frontier truths are within Δdt 0.3s.** Signals are
essentially temporally overlapping. Time-domain separation has
limited potential — only ~19% (Δdt > 0.5s) has any meaningful
temporal slack.

### BB2 — WAV concentration

| bucket | WAVs | % |
|---|---:|---:|
| 1 frontier | 25 | 14.4% |
| 2-3 frontier | 54 | 31.0% |
| 4-6 frontier | 49 | 28.2% |
| 7-10 frontier | 20 | 11.5% |
| 11+ frontier | 26 | 14.9% |

- 174 of 200 WAVs hold frontier entries (mean 5.6/WAV)
- 26 WAVs (15%) hold 11+ frontier entries each — these are crowded
  bands where the capture-effect mechanism dominates
- Max in a single WAV: 23 frontier entries

Any fix must scale to busy bands; 15% of WAVs being "very busy" is
the operationally-relevant regime.

### CC1 — Was the neighbor decoded by pancetta? (pivotal)

For each frontier entry, check if `neighbor_text ∈ pancetta_decoded`:

| category | count | % |
|---|---:|---:|
| **neighbor decoded** | **497** | **51.0%** |
| — truth quieter (textbook SIC victim) | 486 | 49.9% |
| — truth louder (anomaly + decoded) | 11 | 1.1% |
| **neighbor missed** | **477** | **49.0%** |
| — truth louder (dual-miss anomaly) | 227 | 23.3% |
| — truth quieter (weak-pair mutual) | 250 | 25.7% |

**The 51/49 split is the central finding.** Half the frontier is
SIC failure (pancetta decoded the neighbor, subtraction left
residual that didn't surface the weak). Half is dual miss.

These have fundamentally different mechanisms:

- **SIC failure** (486 textbook + 11 anomaly = 497): subtract-aware
  techniques are applicable. Improve `subtract_with_sidelobes`
  residual quality. **hb-218a**.
- **Dual miss** (250 weak-pair + 227 louder anomaly = 477): no
  subtraction to improve. Need joint sync + joint LDPC. **hb-218b**
  (plan-sized).

The AA3 anomaly (238 truths, truth louder) is concentrated in dual
miss (227 of 238) — most "louder-missed" truths are also lost
together with their neighbor, suggesting the louder isn't actually
the dominant signal in those situations. Possibly jt9 SNR estimate
noise; possibly per-bin AGC differential. **hb-218c**.
