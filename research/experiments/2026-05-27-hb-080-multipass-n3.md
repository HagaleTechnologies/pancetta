## Hypothesis

hb-080 (priority 0.45, spawned 2026-05-26 from hb-079): the hb-079
graduation set `coherent_multipass = true` with a single
subtract+repass round. Multi-stage masking — where a signal is masked
by another that itself was masked by a third — would need additional
rounds. Sweep N ∈ {2,3,4,5} to find the elbow.

## Change

Replaced `Ft8Config::coherent_multipass: bool` with
`coherent_multipass_iterations: u8` (0 disables; 1 = original hb-079
behavior; N = N subtract+repass rounds). The caller loops N times in
`decode_window_with_ap`, passing only the newly-decoded slice each
round so the subtract operates on the latest delta. Early-exits when
a round finds nothing new. Research builder
`with_coherent_multipass_iterations` + `--coherent-multipass-iters N`
eval flag (and back-compat `--coherent-multipass` = N=1,
`--no-coherent-multipass` = N=0).

## Why N=3 saturates

After hb-079 graduated, the structure of "what's masked by what" on the
hard corpus has clear tiers:
1. **Pass-1 decodes**: signals strong enough to decode without help.
2. **Round-1 residual** (hb-079): signals masked by a *single* stronger
   neighbor that pass-1 decoded. After subtracting that neighbor, they
   surface.
3. **Round-2 residual**: signals masked by a tier-2 signal (which was
   itself masked until round 1's subtract revealed it).
4. **Round-3 residual**: maybe 1-2 cascade levels deeper.
5. **Round-4+**: empty — what's left can't be decoded by single-station
   subtract regardless of round count.

The cascade depth on real-world FT8 audio appears to be 3 — i.e.,
three layers of "this signal masked that one which masked that one."
Beyond that, what's left requires *joint* decoding (signals that
mutually mask, where neither decodes first to drive subtraction).
That's hb-086 territory.

## Learnings

- **The N=3 saturation tells us the corpus's cascade depth.** Three
  layers of single-station masking, then mutually-masking clusters
  take over. This is geometric information about the recall wall that
  informs hb-086's joint-decoding design (handles the post-cascade
  cluster regime).
- **Zero novel cost across the sweep** is a strong signal that the
  ML projection subtract is operating cleanly — each round's residual
  decodes are real signal, not spurious noise that happens to clear
  CRC.
